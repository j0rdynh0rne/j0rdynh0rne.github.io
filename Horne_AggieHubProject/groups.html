Explore Student Groups

Join a student organization to meet new friends, develop skills, and make a difference on campus!

  • Aggie Robotics Club: Build and program robots for national competitions and workshops.
  • Black Student Union (BSU): Promote cultural awareness and unity across the NC A&T community.
  • Society of Women Engineers (SWE): Support and empower women pursuing engineering and technology careers.
  • Campus Green Initiative: Lead efforts in sustainability, recycling, and environmental advocacy on campus.
  • Aggie Esports Team: Compete in popular games like League of Legends, Valorant, and more in official collegiate tournaments.
  • Entrepreneurship Club: Connect with fellow innovators and receive mentorship on starting your own business or project.
  • NCAT Debate Society: Engage in thought-provoking debates and public speaking events across regional and national platforms.
  • Photography Club: Learn photography skills, edit images, and showcase your work in student exhibitions.
  • Aggie Theatre Group: Participate in stage productions, scriptwriting workshops, and campus theater events.
  • International Student Association (ISA): Celebrate diversity and create a supportive community for international students at NC A&T.
