Active Student Organizations

Society of Women Engineers (SWE)

Empowering women in engineering and technology through professional development, networking, and outreach events. Open to all students in STEM majors.

NCAT Esports Club

Join the Esports Club to compete in popular games like League of Legends, Overwatch, and Rocket League. Weekly tournaments and gaming nights hosted on campus.

Student Government Association (SGA)

The SGA represents the student body, advocating for positive campus changes and organizing student-led events and initiatives.

Black Business Leaders Association (BBLA)

Connect with fellow business students and alumni. Participate in workshops, networking events, and business plan competitions to enhance your leadership skills.
